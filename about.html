Meet Your Divorce Coach

Sarah Johnson

Hello, I'm Sarah Johnson, a certified divorce coach with over 15 years of experience guiding individuals through the challenging journey of divorce. My passion is to empower you to navigate this transition with confidence, clarity, and a sense of renewed purpose.

My Approach

I believe in a holistic approach to divorce coaching, focusing on emotional support, practical guidance, and future planning. My method is rooted in empathy, active listening, and evidence-based strategies to help you:

  • Process emotions and build resilience
  • Develop effective communication strategies
  • Navigate co-parenting challenges
  • Rebuild your personal identity and self-esteem
  • Create a vision for your post-divorce life

As your divorce coach, I commit to:

  • Guide you towards a positive post-divorce future
